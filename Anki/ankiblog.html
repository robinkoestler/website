Work in Cryptography

Currently, I am a Ph.D. student in the Applied Crypto Group at the University of Luxembourg under the supervision of Prof. Jean-Sébastien Coron.

Research Interests

My research interests are in the area of cryptography, in particular in the design and analysis of cryptographic primitives and protocols. I am especially interested in the design of cryptographic primitives for the post-quantum era.
